PROJECT SOMALILAND

WHAT IS SOMALILAND?

Somaliland is a region in the north of Somali that has developed its own democratic government currently run by its fourth president. This region was left devastated by a 30 year civil war with other parts of Somali. This prompted it to try gain recognition as an independent country in 1991. The region is reliant on livestock and crops for the majority of its income and consequently foreign aid plays a very important role.

WHAT WE'RE DOING

Led by Troppodoc and SHiFAT, we will provide basic healthcare to hard to reach areas in Somaliland through clinics aimed at managing the most common treatable diseases in hopes of developing a long-term partnership with these local communities. This will allow us to provide education, promote health, and help in understanding how to assist in developing a sustainable healthcare system. The aim will be to eventually leave with a running self-sustainable healthcare system in areas that had minimal access to healthcare.
